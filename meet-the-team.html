Driven By Students

Founders with a shared mission.

Garments4Goodness was started by students who care about sustainability, dignity, and helping families access clothing essentials.

Mihika Kedia

Hello! I'm Mihika, a rising senior at Milpitas High School. My passion for this club is fueled by my love for sustainability and solving things like clothing insecurity. Some of my hobbies include listening to music and working on cases for speech and debate.

Aadya Rai

Hi! I'm Aadya, a rising senior at Milpitas High School. My passion for helping underprivileged youth and low-income families began ever since I visited a homeless shelter in Milpitas as part of student council in middle school. Some of my other hobbies include participating in speech and debate and volunteering for scouts.

Trishna Erukulla

Hi, I'm Trishna, and going to be a senior at Milpitas High School. After watching my first thrift flip, I knew I wanted to help others find joy in clothes just like me. Some of my hobbies include crocheting, baking, and being a part of my school's debate team.
