Our Mission

Making sustainability easy and accessible.

We give people practical ways to keep clothing in use, reduce waste, and support their communities. Our work is grounded in access, education, community action, and responsible reuse.

Accessible action Community impact Sustainability education Reduce, reuse, recycle

What We Do

Initiatives that turn awareness into action.

Door-to-door collections

We organize neighborhood clothing donation drives that make giving convenient and help usable garments reach people who need them.

Community donation boxes

We set up clothing donation boxes at schools, businesses, and community spaces so people can contribute close to home.

Social media education

Our social media team shares practical sustainability education and has generated more than 60,000 impressions.

Sustainability workshops

We host workshops that help people understand clothing waste and learn how to reduce, reuse, and recycle what they wear.
