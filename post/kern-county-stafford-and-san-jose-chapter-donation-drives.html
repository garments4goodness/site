Kern County, Stafford, and San Jose Chapter donation drives!

We are so excited to announce the recent success of g4g! In 4 months, we have spread awareness to 64K people, donated over 2.1K articles of clothing with estimated worth $21,000 to underprivileged families, opened 25+ chapters in 9 different states across the nation, and landed 7 corporate sponsorships, including Chipotle, Panda Express, and Yogurtland!

Our second drive was conducted by our g4g chapter in Kern County, California. At Independence High School, they received around 200 total articles of clothing in donation!

Our third drive was conducted by our g4g chapter in Stafford, Virginia at Brooke Point High School.

Our fourth drive was conducted by our g4g chapter in San Jose, California. They got around 400 total articles of clothing in donation from their drive in Leigh High School!

We are so proud of our chapters and officers for making so much progress in the last few months, and look forward to your continued support!
